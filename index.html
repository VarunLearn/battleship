Rules of the Game

  • There are 8 total ships.
  • Each boat consists of either 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.
  • There are both horizontal and vertical boats for 4, 3, and 2-blocks.
  • The 5-block ship is rotated randomly.
  • o Means hit.
  • x Means miss.
  • | Shows location of ships when you lose.
